We, Yang Wei Laboratory, major in terrestrial environment remote sensing.

The research subject is:

Quantitative satellite remote sensing in terrestrial ecosystems (mainly vegetation and lakes) and related research;

Estimating vegetation and lake biological and physical parameters using satellite data;

To discover, interpret, and model natural phenomena (for example, photosynthesis) based on satellite observations.
